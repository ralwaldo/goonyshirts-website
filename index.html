Our approach

Clothing that makes you think before you get dressed.

Every Goonyshirt begins with a big idea — a question that has rattled around the skulls of the greatest thinkers in history. We then print it on a really comfortable shirt.

We believe philosophy is too important to be locked in academia. Bring Nietzsche to the farmer's market. Take Camus to the gym. Let Wittgenstein ruin your dinner party.

"The more I read, the more I acquire, the more certain I am that I know nothing — which is why I buy another shirt."
— Voltaire (paraphrased, liberally)

We exist because ideas deserve to be worn.

Philosophy is not an ivory tower exercise. It is the original punk. It questions authority, disturbs comfort, and insists on honesty in a world of convenient fictions.

Goonyshirts was born from the belief that the best conversations happen when something on your chest makes a stranger stop and think. Or laugh. Or argue with you at a bus stop.

We work with ethically sourced fabrics, local printers, and big, uncomfortable ideas. Each shirt is a provocation, gently laundered.
